About Saveetha Engineering College

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), established in 2001, is a premier institution committed to providing world-class technical education. We strive to nurture competent and compassionate engineers who can contribute to societal progress. Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty team.

Our Departments

Computer Science and Engineering

Focuses on algorithms, data structures, software development, and artificial intelligence.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Covers circuit design, signal processing, telecommunications, and embedded systems.

Mechanical Engineering

Emphasizes design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, and robotics.

Civil Engineering

Deals with structural analysis, construction management, and environmental engineering.

Information Technology

Concentrates on software engineering, networking, cybersecurity, and data science.
